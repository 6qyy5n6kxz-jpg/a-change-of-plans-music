Festival and community-event FAQ

Is the performance family-friendly?

The set can be planned around recognizable music and an audience-appropriate community setting.

Can you perform outdoors?

Yes, when a suitable stage or performance area, power, weather plan, and equipment protection are available.

Do you provide sound?

We can provide sound for many events. Larger or technically specific events are reviewed with the organizer before quoting.

Do you travel outside Northwest Ohio?

Travel may be possible depending on the date and event. Distance and related costs are included in the quote.
