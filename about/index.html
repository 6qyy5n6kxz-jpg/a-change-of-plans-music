Meet the duo

Two distinct voices, one adaptable performance.

A Change Of Plans is Devin Frank and Kendra German, a Northwest Ohio duo arranging familiar music around piano, guitar, and two lead vocalists.

Devin Frank

Vocals, piano, and guitar

Devin builds each arrangement around the needs of the song and event, moving between piano and guitar for variety. His approach emphasizes recognizable songs, strong vocals, and arrangements that feel familiar without being predictable.

Kendra German

Featured lead vocalist

Kendra brings a strong, expressive vocal presence central to the duo’s live identity. Her range helps A Change Of Plans cover pop, rock, country, and classic favorites while keeping the performance engaging and audience-focused.

Together, Devin and Kendra can move from quiet dinner music to sing-along favorites, church special music, wedding moments, and energetic community events.

Our approach

One Band. Multiple Experiences.

A Change Of Plans was built to be flexible. Rather than creating separate bands for every musical style, Devin Frank and Kendra German develop curated Signature Shows under one trusted name. Each show brings its own theme, repertoire and personality while maintaining the vocals, arrangements and professionalism audiences expect from A Change Of Plans.

How it comes together

Arrangements shaped for the setting

Instrument changes, two vocal styles, and an adjustable sound setup make it possible to serve different rooms without losing the duo’s identity. The goal is a polished performance that connects with the audience and respects the event around it.
