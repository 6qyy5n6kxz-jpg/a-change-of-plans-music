Signature Show

Women of Country

What to Expect

Women of Country delivers strong lead vocals and familiar country favorites in A Change Of Plans' distinctive style. You'll hear recognizable choruses, authentic instrumentation, and engaging arrangements that celebrate the women who've shaped country music.

Whether presented as an intimate acoustic duo or expanded with guest musicians, the focus remains on delivering the energy and artistry these songs deserve.
