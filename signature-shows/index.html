Signature Shows

A Change Of Plans offers curated musical experiences designed for wineries, festivals, community concerts, private events, restaurants, churches and other venues throughout Northwest Ohio and Southeast Michigan.

Each Signature Show brings familiar songs to life through thoughtfully curated themes, combining strong vocals, piano, acoustic guitar and engaging arrangements that can scale from an intimate duo performance to an expanded show with guest musicians.
