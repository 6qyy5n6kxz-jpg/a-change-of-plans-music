Signature Show

90s Acoustic Rewind

What to Expect

90s Acoustic Rewind captures the spirit of an era through acoustic guitar, piano, and vocals. Songs you remember are presented in A Change Of Plans' thoughtful arrangements—not as a costume party, but as a celebration of the music itself.

Whether you're reconnecting with songs from your past or discovering 1990s favorites for the first time, this show creates moments of nostalgia and recognition.
