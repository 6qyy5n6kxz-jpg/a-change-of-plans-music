Frequently asked questions

Helpful details before you inquire

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Northwest Ohio and regularly serve Toledo, Genoa, Oak Harbor, Port Clinton, Fremont, Bowling Green, and nearby communities. Travel beyond the standard area may add a fee.

Do you provide your own sound system?

We can provide an organized sound system appropriate for many indoor and outdoor settings. We will review venue size, power, and production needs with you.

Can we request specific songs?

Yes. Share important requests early so we can confirm fit and preparation needs. Requests are not automatically guaranteed.

Is a solo performer available?

Devin is available for select solo bookings with vocals and piano or guitar. It is a secondary option subject to his schedule.
