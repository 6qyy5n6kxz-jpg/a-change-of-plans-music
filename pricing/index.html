Frequently asked questions

Helpful details before you inquire

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Northwest Ohio and regularly serve Toledo, Genoa, Oak Harbor, Port Clinton, Fremont, Bowling Green, and nearby communities. Travel beyond the standard area may add a fee.

Do you provide your own sound system?

We can provide an organized sound system appropriate for many indoor and outdoor settings. We will review venue size, power, and production needs with you.

Can you perform indoors and outdoors?

Yes, when the performance area, weather plan, power, and equipment protection are suitable. Outdoor details are confirmed before booking.

Can we request specific songs?

Yes. Share important requests early so we can confirm fit and account for preparation. Requests are not automatically guaranteed.

Do you perform ceremonies and cocktail hours?

Those are available event options. Tell us which portions you are planning so we can recommend a setup and quote.

Can you adjust volume for dinner events?

Yes. The sound can be set to support conversation or bring more energy when the setting calls for it.

How far in advance should we book?

Availability varies, so earlier is helpful—especially for weddings and popular dates. It is still worth asking about nearer dates.

Do you perform solo and as a duo?

Yes. The inquiry form lets you select solo, duo, or ask for a recommendation.

How long can you perform?

Performance length is planned around the event type and schedule. Share your desired duration for a tailored recommendation.

Do you travel outside Northwest Ohio?

Travel may be possible depending on the date and event. Distance and related costs are considered in the quote.
